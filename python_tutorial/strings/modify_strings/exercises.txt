What is a correct syntax to print a string in upper case letters?
    'Welcome'.upper()

Return the string without any whitespaces at the beginning or the end.
    txt = " Hello World "
    txt = txt.strip()

Convert the value of txt to upper case.
    txt = "Hello World"
    txt = txt.upper()

Convert the value of txt to lower case.
    txt = "Hello World"
    txt = txt.lower()

Replace the charecter H with a J.
    txt = "Hello World"
    txt = txt.replace("H", "J")
